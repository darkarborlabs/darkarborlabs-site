Who we are

About the Studio

Dark Arbor Labs LLC is an independent software engineering and technology consulting firm. We operate in two modes: building software products for direct market distribution, and advising clients who want the depth and experience of a major global consultancy — delivered directly, without the overhead, and with the flexibility to move at the speed the work actually requires.

The studio is founded and led by a technology executive with 20 years of enterprise consulting experience, most recently leading the data and AI practice at a national consultancy in Los Angeles. That background informs how we approach every project — with a focus on what's actually useful over what's technically impressive, and a clear-eyed view of where complexity is warranted versus where it gets in the way.

We're based in Los Angeles and work with clients locally and remotely.
